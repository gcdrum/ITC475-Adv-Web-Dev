Block Break game rules

  • There are 112 blocks to break!

  • If the ball drops below the paddle, you will lose a life.

  • Each player gets 3 lives to break all the blocks by deflecting the balls trajectory back up into the blocks or off the side walls.

  • Use the "left" and "right" arrow keys to slide horizontially.

  • Press the spacebar to drop the ball from the top of the game area when you are ready to begin a life.

  • Head over to the highscores page to see how your score ranks in the top 10 from scores stored in the database!
